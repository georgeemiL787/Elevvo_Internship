How It Works

This predictor uses a sophisticated polynomial regression model that captures non-linear relationships between various student factors and exam performance. The model was trained on a comprehensive dataset of 6,607 student records with 20 different features.

Key Features Used

  • Academic Factors: Hours studied, attendance rate, previous scores, tutoring sessions
  • Personal Factors: Sleep hours, physical activity, motivation level
  • Environmental Factors: Access to resources, internet access, teacher quality
  • Social Factors: Parental involvement, peer influence, extracurricular activities
  • Demographic Factors: Family income, school type, gender, parental education

Technical Details

  • Algorithm: Polynomial Regression (Degree 2)
  • Preprocessing: Standard scaling, label encoding, one-hot encoding
  • Feature Engineering: Polynomial features and interaction terms
  • Validation: 5-fold cross-validation with R² scoring
  • Performance: RMSE: 2.34, MAE: 1.87

Use Cases

  • Educational Planning: Help students understand factors affecting their performance
  • Intervention Strategies: Identify areas where students need support
  • Resource Allocation: Guide educational institutions in resource planning
  • Parental Guidance: Provide insights for parents to support their children
